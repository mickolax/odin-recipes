Detroit Hot Honey Wings

Description

These honey wings are sweet and spicy and amazing on the grill. This is a Detroit recipe, so enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds chicken wings, tips discarded
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper (add more if you can stand it)
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 cup honey, divided
  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • ½ cup hot sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill to medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Wash wings well and pat dry with paper towels. Season wings with cayenne, salt, and pepper.
  3. Cook chicken wings on the preheated grill, brushing wings liberally with 1/2 cup honey while cooking, until cooked through and juices run clear, 20 to 30 minutes.
  4. Pour melted butter into a large bowl; mix in hot sauce and remaining 1/2 cup honey.
  5. Remove wings from the grill and immediately toss them in hot honey butter sauce to coat. Serve wings wet or return them to the grill for 1 minute per side to set sauce.
